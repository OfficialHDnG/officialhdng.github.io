[ 4 전설 가요 ] PLAY


Shone:Strike Power Calculation is based on member’s hidden Strength stat, with X and Roars leading in Strength. This stat can be boosted in the future by Glyptic carving
Nyeve:Empire Power Calculation is based on current HP. Heal your members to full health to deal highest damage from Nyeve:Empire
Nyeve MASSIVE Combo: GoldenAge can play a 2nd Nyeve: Empire, which deals extra damage. After that, subsequent Nyeve LoreSongs deal minimal damage
Healon:Calm Power Calculation is based on missing HP. The lower HP a member is, the stronger Healon:Calm becomes.
Healon MASSIVE Combo: X can play a 2nd Healon:Calm, which deals extra damage.  After that, subsequent Healon LoreSongs deal minimal damage
Combo Defense: when defending, you can choose to not defend against the 1st Nyeve or Healon LoreSongs, and only against the 2nd to avoid the 4x damage
Boss Manipulation by Healon: Healon can be played to get rid of Shone LoreSongs that Bosses might have. This makes the Boss less likely to attack you with Shone:Strike, important for high strength Bosses

[ 불멸의 정신 ] 4th Friend


Spirit Build: The 4th member who is left out teaches the entire team a global skill: Spirit. Spirit summons the 4th member whenever the team plays the 4th member's LoreSong.
Spirit Power Calculation: Spirit randomly strike an enemy; the power is boosted based on how many LoreSongs of the 4th member remains

[제일이다] SECRET ULTIMATE

The 4 members each have a secret ultimate that can charge up. !Each time a Secret Ultimate is launched, it consumes all the members' Secret Ultimate charges.
If you do not choose to use the light {Ult!} abilities, each member can launch a unique ultimate ability:

X: combines the 1st two elements of Ocean & Wind for a new ultimate. X charges up his Secret Ultimate each time a player is striked or healed to an even-number HP. When launched, X's Secret Ultimate strikes all enemies multiple times, including strikes for % HP. As an Ocean-Wind power, this is weakened by Blaze & StarEarth songs X holds.

GoldenAge: combines the elements of Thunder & Light for a new ultimate. GoldenAge gains a new LoreSong: Judgment, which strikes 1 target for massive damage, including % of the damage target has dealt. GoldenAge charges up his Secret Ultimate whenever a player on the field did not do any damage. As a Thunder-Light power, this is weakened by Eden songs GoldenAge holds.

Roars: combines the elements of Blaze & StarEarth for a new ultimate. Roars charges up his Secret Ultimate each time Roars is damaged. When launched, Roars deals 3 random strikes, the third strike creating star-strikes that reflect off the remaining enemies. As a Blaze-StarEarth power, this is weakened by Ocean & Wind songs Roars holds.

Airson: uses the last, mysterious element, Eden, for a new ultimate. Airson charges up his Secret Ultimate each time a player gains a LoreSong, or receives a gift of a LoreSong, or via Recycle: when players return a LoreSong beyond the LoreSong save limit. When launched, Airson strikes all enemies and heals all team members 7 times. As an Eden-elemental power, this power is not weakened but boosted by all other 6 elemental songs Airson holds.

In the Secret Script Hall, you can go to Script, Setting, Secret Ult Progress to toggle a mode that always notifies when you successfully charged a members Ultimate.

Win condition is to defeat the center Boss. To help you, Roars, Airson, GoldenAge, and X gains the following special abilities:
EndGame: When you have defeated a piece (left or right) of the boss, each time a Secret Ultimate is launched, a following member will join right after to combo. For example, X follows GoldenAge, follows Airson, follows Roars, who follows back to X HeavenSea.
Critical Mode: When a boss has both pieces (left and right) overcome, a low-HP member can enter Critical Mode, which allows them to quickly charge their Secret Ultimate!

[ 칠행 상생 ] ELEMENTS

Based on the 오방색, the 7 elements, in order from {Origin} to {Mystery}, are: Ocean, Wind, Thunder, Light, StarEarth, Blaze, Eden.
The Eden elemental LoreSongs allows X to always win against his target when playing {X: The Mystery}, unless the target also plays an Eden LoreSong.
Immunity, Healing, and Weakness: shifting elemental modes can grant a member immunity, heal, or strength against Bosses through elemental weakness
SkyWar Strike: in new Gem Modes, Airson and Roars gain SkyWar. SkyWar can be played with infinite range and infinite combos, and take advantage of elemental weakness, immunity, and healing
SkyWar Defense: when a SkyWar is launched, the two attacker and target take turns playing Shone:Strike back and forth. The player who can no longer counter with a Shone:Strike is damaged. This can be used to expend a Boss of Shone:Strikes. 
LoreSong Saving: remember only 4 LoreSongs can be saved at the end of each turn - though GoldenAge and Airson can gift LoreSongs back and forth to escape this limit

The World of Ri [璃 · 리] is a complex, multi-layered, post-apocalyptic world consisting of three components:

  1. "Pinnacle of Man-Kings" [万国巅 · 만국전]
  2. The Wild [野 · 야]
  3. “Yemog” [蕊牧 · 예목]

"Pinnacle of Man-Kings" [万国巅 · 만국전]

All of the visible population in Ri is concentrated here, often referred to simply as "The City". In a landmass less than 0.3% of its original size, roughly 300 nations with a sum of 10 billion human beings are condensed into mixed-use skyscrapers linked by high-speed rail in the skies. The average height of buildings is 314.7 stories high. "The City" was created by humanity in response to The Apocalypse, which gave rise to The Wild. The mastermind behind the architectural concentration is Elronde: Majesty, non-candidate.


The Wild [野 · 야]

"The Wild" is the vast region surrounding the core "City", extending all the way to what the inhabitants refer to as the edge of the world. The Wild is overrun by a form of CTVT that became the first dog-to-human case of transmission in history. Humanity's response was the condense themselves into an inner urban core by vertical architecture as opposed to fighting head-on with the CTVT that over years is presumed to have also cross the fauna-flora barrier to metastasize to plant organisms. In all, The Wild is an off-limits area that covers over 99.7% of the original landmass. Despite this, the Wild is home to one single individual: Healon, Candidate of X.


“Yemog” [蕊牧 · 예목]

“Yemog” is an unknown realm that purportedly exists on top of the entire human civilization. Those who have visited "Yemog" claim the realm is an untouched state of nature that is in stark contrast from both "Pinnacle of Man-Kings" and the cancerous "Wild". Purportedly inhabited by titanic beings who are able to "create entire worlds with their voices". Little else is known about "Yemog", and the strongest evidence of its existence is the trace powers that people unlock after visiting and returning to the human world. Colloquially, "Yemog" is also used to refer to specific individuals who have visited the mysterious region and returned with its powers. The first Yemog revealed is Nyeve.
