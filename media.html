!Beta Warning: these game mechanics are always being tested and developed - if you experience a bug, please contact MCsL staff!
The game uses 3 characters as 1 intricate machine, and allows “build paths” in each Boss battle
Order of Action & Stage Position: You can determine your 3 members’ order of action by highlighting them in any order you wish

[ 4 전설 가요 ] PLAY


Shone:Strike Power Calculation: is based on member’s hidden STRENGTH stat, with X and Roars leading in STRENGTH. This stat can be boosted by GLYPTIC carving
Nyeve:Empire Power Calculation: is based on current HP. Heal your members to full health to deal highest damage from Nyeve:Empire
Nyeve MASSIVE Combo: GoldenAge can play a 2nd Nyeve: Empire, which deals 4x damage. After that, subsequent Nyeve LoreSongs deal minimal damage
Healon:Calm Power Calculation: is based on missing HP. The lower HP a member is, the stronger Healon:Calm becomes.
Healon MASSIVE Combo: X can play a 2nd Healon:Calm, which deals 4x damage.  After that, subsequent Healon LoreSongs deal minimal damage
Combo Defense: when defending, you can choose to not defend against the 1st Nyeve or Healon, and only against the 2nd to avoid the 4x damage
Boss Manipulation by Healon: Healon can be played to get rid of Shone LoreSongs that Bosses may hold. This makes the Boss less likely to attack you with Shone:Strike, important for strong physical-strength Bosses

[ 불멸의 정신 ] 4th Friend


Spirit Build: The 4th member who is left out teaches the entire team a global skill: SPIRIT. Spirit summons the 4th member whenever the team plays their LoreSong.
Spirit Power Calculation: Spirit randomly strike an enemy; the power is boosted based on how many Home LoreSongs remain

[제일이다] ULTIMATE


Core-Reveal: Bosses can become stronger against ultimates when they are the only surviving core
Ult Damage Calculation: although all LoreSongs must be played, only LoreSongs from members’ Lore-Based locations contribute to its power:
Roars - Pinnacle of ManKings
🜎Airson - the Slums ⚶
GoldenAge - Yemog ♛
X - CTVT in the Wild ❃

GoldenAge secret ult boost: GoldenAge’s ability, which allows him to gift a LoreSong each turn, boosts team members’ ults - Roars Ult is powered by Pinnacle of ManKings 🜎; Airson Ult is powered by Slums ⚶, and X Ult is powered by CTVT in the Wild ❃

[ 칠행 상생 ] ELEMENTS


Immunity, Healing, and Weakness: shifting elemental modes can grant a member immunity, heal, or strength against Bosses through elemental weakness
SkyWar Strike: in new Power modes, Airson and Roars gain SkyWar. SkyWar can be played with infinite range and infinite combos, and take advantage of elemental weakness, immunity, and healing
SkyWar Defense: when a SkyWar is launched, the two attacker and target take turns playing Shone:Strike back and forth. The player who cannot defend with a Shone:Strike is damaged. This can be used to expend a Boss of the Shone:Strikes. 
LoreSong Saving: remember only 4 LoreSongs can be saved at the end of each turn - though GoldenAge and Airson can gift LoreSongs back and forth to escape this limit

The World of Ri [璃 · 리] is a complex, multi-layered, post-apocalyptic world consisting of three components:

  1. "Pinnacle of Man-Kings" [万国巅 · 만국전]
  2. The Wild [野 · 야]
  3. “Yemog” [蕊牧 · 예목]

"Pinnacle of Man-Kings" [万国巅 · 만국전]

All of the visible population in Ri is concentrated here, often referred to simply as "The City". In a landmass less than 0.3% of its original size, roughly 300 nations with a sum of 10 billion human beings are condensed into mixed-use skyscrapers linked by high-speed rail in the skies. The average height of buildings is 314.7 stories high. "The City" was created by humanity in response to The Apocalypse, which gave rise to The Wild. The mastermind behind the architectural concentration is Elronde: Majesty, non-candidate.


The Wild [野 · 야]

"The Wild" is the vast region surrounding the core "City", extending all the way to what the inhabitants refer to as the edge of the world. The Wild is overrun by a form of CTVT that became the first dog-to-human case of transmission in history. Humanity's response was the condense themselves into an inner urban core by vertical architecture as opposed to fighting head-on with the CTVT that over years is presumed to have also cross the fauna-flora barrier to metastasize to plant organisms. In all, The Wild is an off-limits area that covers over 99.7% of the original landmass. Despite this, the Wild is home to one single individual: Healon, Candidate of X.


“Yemog” [蕊牧 · 예목]

“Yemog” is an unknown realm that purportedly exists on top of the entire human civilization. Those who have visited "Yemog" claim the realm is an untouched state of nature that is in stark contrast from both "Pinnacle of Man-Kings" and the cancerous "Wild". Purportedly inhabited by titanic beings who are able to "create entire worlds with their voices". Little else is known about "Yemog", and the strongest evidence of its existence is the trace powers that people unlock after visiting and returning to the human world. Colloquially, "Yemog" is also used to refer to specific individuals who have visited the mysterious region and returned with its powers. The first Yemog revealed is Nyeve.
